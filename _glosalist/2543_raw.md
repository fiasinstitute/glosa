Hallo all, 

Now also issues 42-71 of Plu Glosa Nota, the Glosa newspaper, can
be read online. Please have a look at www.glosa.org/pgn .

the scanning work.

It is the biggest chunk of Glosa stuff going online on one single day
ever, I believe.
