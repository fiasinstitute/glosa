Plu Glosa Nota


"Plu Glosa Nota" (PGN) pa eqa un ofici Glosa nova-papira. Id habe un internatio numera ISSN 0265-6892. Norma, 4 papira singu anua pa gene grafo.     "Plu Glosa Nota" (PGN) was the official Glosa newspaper. It has the international number ISSN 0265-6892. Normally 4 issues a year had been written.
 
Tem 2014 un ultima PGN pa gene publika. Id habe u numera 97.     The last issue, number 97, had been published 2014.
 
Oligo artikla pote gene lekto in inter-reti. Vide e klik un indexi infra.
Plus tu pote gene uno holo, ge-skana PGN homo PDF-dokumenta. 		    A few articles can be read online. See and click the index below.
There are also some complete, scanned issues as PDF-file available.
 
Glosa autori Wendy Ashby dona uno origi fonta-dokumenta de fe pro uti id in inter-reti: pgn086.doc, pgn087.doc, pgn088.doc, pgn089.doc (MS-Word-forma, minus imagi e enigma-pagina. HTML-versio vide infra) e portmek.doc (Portuguese Glosa mekanici). Mega gratia a fe!     Glosa author Wendy Ashby donated some of her original source-files for the use in the WWW: pgn086.doc, pgn087.doc, pgn088.doc, pgn089.doc (MS-Word-format, without images and riddle-page. HTML-version see below.) and portmek.doc (Portuguese Glosa mechanics). Many thanks to her.
 
Gratia a Edmund Grimley Evans (ex UK) de provide e skana plu numera 41-43+45-71 e a José Antonio Vergara (ex Chile) de plu numera 19+21+27+29+30.     Thanks to Edmund Grimley Evans (from UK) for providing and scanning issues 41-43+45-71 and to José Antonio Vergara (from Chile) for issues 19+21+27+29+30.
