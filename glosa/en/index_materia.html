Learning- and Textmaterial

  • “18 Steps to Fluency in Euro-Glosa” (190 kB HTML + 143 kB GIF = 333 kB)
    The official description of Glosa in the form of a childrens's school book and at the same time the best way to learn Glosa. A German (also as 737 kB pdf-document) and a Russian translation is available.
  • Glosa Basic Reference (122 kB)
    A Glosa starting kit.    Contents: short description Glosa, “grammar”, operator words, rules, pronounciation, orthography, examples, vocabulary English-Glosa and Glosa-English.
    (Also available in German translation “Grundwissen Glosa”).
  • “Glosa Internet Dictionary” (GID) Glosa-English (352 kB)
    (also as plain ASCII-file glen.txt and a “Basi”(basic)-version of preferable words) and
    Englisch-Glosa (368 kB) (or engl.txt).
    The most comprehensive Glosa dictionary.
  • GID-Index (with further Glosa-dictionaries and information about them and the Glosa words)
  • A Glosa grammar (a draft by Robin Gaskell. Also available in Russian.)
  • A Glosa grammar (notes by Carmelo Mico Ranibakt, when he was learning Glosa.)
  • Seminar 1 about Glosa's word order (syntax)
    (Also available a translation into German. Nearly identical is the text “word order”.)
  • Seminar 2 about verbs in Glosa (and in German Seminar 2)
  • Writing Hints
  • History Behind Glosa (pdf-file), about the history of the language, in English.
  • introduction to Glosa in French
  • introduction to Glosa in Spanish
  • Short information in other languages (Dutch, French, Hungarian, Russian).
  • Glosa Mechanics Glosa-English-Portuguese (a file in MS-Word-format only, sorry)
  • A little computer program, that helps to translate from and into Glosa.
  • The book “Interglossa” (pdf-file) by Lancelot Hogben, 1943 (Interglossa is the forerunner of Glosa).
     
  • PGN archive (some articles of “Plu Glosa Nota”, the official Glosa newspaper)
  • A Texts's Collection
    Texts stolen from other Glosa websites (see link list) put together in one file, some pictures, some German supplement. Contains also photos of the Glosa inventors.
  • Sample texts Glosa-Spanish parallel
  • Un Hedo Prince (by Oscar Wilde, Glosa by Wendy Ashby)
  • L'élixir du Révérend Père Gaucher (by Alphonse Daudet, in French and Glosa)
  • Roberti (a little story, written and illustrated by Syd Pidd, only Glosa)
  • Pusi Glosa Kristo-Bibli (some Bible texts)
  • Bahá'i Texts (some texts to the Bahá'i religion, English and Glosa parallel)
  • Bhagavad Gita (small part of a central text of Hinduism in Glosa)
  • Qo akti? (a “video installation” by the Netherlands artist Mathilde ter Heijne. Description of the project, a critique and the script. Partly English, German, Glosa.)
  • Listening Samples (in MP3-files. Sorry, a very low sound quality, so giving only a rude impression.)
  • Justi (a newspaper/newsletter in Esperanto and Glosa)
